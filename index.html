About

Welcome! I am WANG Bokang, a final yeal computer science student at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and expect to graduate on May 2020. I am a passionate game designer and programmer.


I love playing games and making games so I have formed a four-person indie game development team with my friends. I believe that game is the ninth category of art. I hope my games can make players have a strong emotional experience and inspire them.


You can find my works below and more information at the top right corner. Please feel free to contact me by bokang.wang@connect.polyu.hk if you require any further information.
