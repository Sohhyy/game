Introduction

Puppet Master is a 2D puzzle game I developed during Weekly Game Jam- Week 79 on itch.io, under the theme of “Puppet Master”. I am the gameplay programmer in our team. For this theme, We can have two understandings of "master". The puppet master is also the master of the puppet. The story is about a puppet with a soul wants to rescue its evil master. The puppet can only walk in one direction and the player needs to indirectly control the puppet to escape from the traps and reach the stairs. There are totally 13 well-designed levels. And there are two different endings. We also tried to add some features of the mata-game in this game.

Notable Features

  • Nice designed puzzles.
  • Two different endings.
  • Control the main character indirectly.
  • Experimented with meta-game elements.
