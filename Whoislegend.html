Introduction

Who is legend is a 2D 2-player action game I developed during GMTK Game Jam 2019, under the theme of “only one”. And I am responsible for all programming works. In this game, two players need to both compete and cooperate. The background of this game is that zombie crisis breaks out and two players are the only humans alive in the world. But they are all infected. They need to fight for the only one cure. And at last, only one person can get the cure and survive. However, there are many enemy zombies and two players need to fight for them together. We also designed some doors. Two players can open the door easily but one player need to take a long time open the door. The cure will appear at the end of the game. The player can kill the other player at anytime of the game. And the dead player will become a powerful zombie. herefore, the player need to make decisions on when to cooperate with the other player and when to kill the other player. We also learned from parkour game and make this game more exciting.

Notable Features

  • Learned from parkour game to make this game more exciting.
  • Both compete and cooperate with the other player.
