Introduction

This music is the course project of "Creative Digital Media Design". Music is quite important in the game. Good music can not only convey the emotions but also increase the immersion of the game. Due to my desire to learn music composition, I took this course. The class taught us how to use the software to compose the music. However, music composition needs a solid background of music theory. So I watched a lot of online videos to learn the basic music theory. And finally, I successfully made this music based on a given motif and theme.

Design

The theme of this music is March. March is the month of transition from winter to spring. No matter how cold it is in winter, as soon as spring comes, everything will be better. At the beginning of the music, the rhythm is slow, which represents the winter. In the middle part of the song, the rhythm gradually becomes faster. Also, emotion is gradually higher. The whole piece come to an end in the happiest part. It means spring comes. I want to use this song to convey an optimistic attitude towards life. No matter how difficult life is now, as long as you persist, success will surely come.
