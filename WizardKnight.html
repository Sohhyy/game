Introduction

Wizard Knight is a 3D TPS game which is the course project of "Game Design and Development". It is also the first game I made. It contains both single-player mode and Multiplayer online mode. For single-player mode, the player needs to control a wizard to defeat waves of monsters, explore the map and finally defeat the boss. For Multiplayer online mode, there are multiple players in the multiplayer arena battle. Players are supposed to kill other rivals as well as protect himself and try to get the highest kill/death ratio.

I am the gameplay programmer and level designer of single player mode. Since I am a big fan of Dark Souls, I refer to a lot of elements of Dark Souls and make this TPS game contain many RPG elements. By developing this game, I learn a lot about the flow of developing a game and get familiar with Unity Engine.
