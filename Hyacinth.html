GGJ2019

Introduction

Hyacinth is a 2D puzzle game we developed during Global Game Jam 2019 Shenzhen Site , under the theme of “What home means to you?” It tells a story about family conflicts in a child’s view. I believe everyone suffer from the family conflicts to some extend. We wanted to use this game attract people’s more attention to this issue.

Design

This game reflects a common social phenomenon: how dysfunctional family relationship brings harm to children. The idea came from our own experience. I always believe that personal experience is a perfect source of creation. This game tells a story indiretly because we combined gameplay with narrative. We wanted to bring players a traumatized experience and arouse their empathy and sympathy.

In this game, the blue circle stands for father, the red circle stands for mother and the orange circle stands for the child. There are three scenes for each level, the crosses in the left and right scenes reprsent the conflicts of parents.The player need to connect father and mother without passing though the crosses. The process of players solving puzzles represents the child trying to reconcile family conflicts, which let the players strongly feel children’s helpless, fear and anger.

We learned from many famous games such as Florence, Undertale and The Witness. We conbined the advantages of those game with our own ideas. Although the theme of this game is sad, but this game tells a hopeful story. At the beginning, the child in the game suffered from the pain which brought by parental conflicts. He tried to resolve conflicts but failed. He felt so helpless and depressed. At this time, a girl appeared in his world. The girl saved this boy with love. Finally, they get married and have a happy family.

My Contributions

  • As a programmer, I implemented all the programming works such as the gameplay and UI.
  • As a designer, I participated in the design of the story.
  • As a teamleader, I was responsible for the project management. Making a game in 48 hours is a really chanllenging job. My solution was dividing the whole project into small tasks and set a deadline for each task. This is a really useful method to control the progression of the project. Also, I made a detailed schedule on the priority lists for all art and sound assets so that we can work more efficiently. Finally, we successfully developed this game in limited time.

Notable Experience

  • Completed a full game production cycle within 48 hours.
  • Combined the gameplay with narrative and successfully struck a responsive chord in the hearts of players.
  • Met many indie game developers and became friends with them.
