Introduction

This is the Visual Story course project, under the theme "interacive story/ non-linear story". We designed and developed this 2D interactive story game focusing on the relationship between LGBT community and their parents. The story's outline is, on Valentine’s day, a girl plans to date with her girlfriend. Her parents, however, haven’t noticed their relationship and wished she could date with a guy. The girl had a wonderful time while dating. She wants to keep the secret, but her parents happen to find their photo at home. When she comes home at night, they start quarreling...
