Introduction

This is the Buliding Virtual Worlds Round3 project, under the theme "fun". Another requirement is no controller. We only have one week for this project. Therefore, we decided to make a two-player cooperation game to make our game more fun. In this game , each player is connected with a Vive tracker on the waist. They need to move around to control the two-head dog. When their distance becomes farther, the hotdog will be stretched. When their distance becomes shorter, the hotdog will also become shorter. They need to work together to collect the good items and dodge bad items.

My Contributions

  • As a programmer, I finished the first playable prototype in 3 hours.
  • As a programmer, I implemented the character controls, random item generation, item collision detection and the whole game flow.
