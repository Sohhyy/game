Volunteer

Introduction

In 9-10 November 2019, I volunteered in the 9th G-Fusion Game Show in Guangzhou. It was hold by one of the most influential game media in China - Gamecores. Many big game companies participated in the show such as Xbox, Ubisoft, Tencent Games, NetEase Games. Many indie game developers also came to promote their indie games. Also, the organizer invited American actor Roger Clark, who provided the voice and motion capture for Arthur Morgan in Red Dead Redemption 2, and Japanese game producer, the father of the Castlevania Koji Igarashi to give a talk.

My Job

I volunteered at Tencent A.C.E Program. This program aims to help small game studios and indie game developers to release their game on the mobile platform. I was responsible for promoting the mobile version of the Slay The Spire. My job was to introduce the game to players, maintain order and hand out some gifts to attract more people. Although volunteering was very tiring, it was very meaningful. Not only did I make friends with my enthusiastic teammates, but also I had a deeper connection with the game industry.

Notable Experience —— A Talk With Mr. Ryan Clark

This experience provided me with a very good opportunity to get in contact with players, game companies, game media, and indie game developers. The founder and CEO of Brace Yourself Games, who is also the designer of the Crypt of the NecroDancer and Cadence of Hyrule Mr. Ryan Clark also came to the show to promote their new game Phantom Brigade. I am very happy I can have a short talk with him. He provided a lot of very useful suggestions for entering the game industry. He emphasized that nowadays game companies have high requirements for practitioners' ability. If you want to be competitive, you must use your works to speak. He suggested that practice is the best way to improve ability. Keep making games and especially working on some big projects are very helpful. When asked about the secret of the success of his games, his answer was the unique gameplay. He mentioned that the most important thing about the game is to be interesting. But it is also the hardest thing to achieve. Unique gameplay can make a game interesting and stand out from the competitors. This valuable conversation has benefited me a lot and made me reflect on what I still lack to accomplish my dream.
